# Student & parent guide

Students and parents share the `/student` portal. Parents see their child's bus and attendance; students see
their own.

Portal: `/student` · Sign in as `parent1` or `student1` / `Password@123`.

## Your sections

| Section | Route | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dashboard | `/student` | Today's overview |
| Live tracking | `/student/tracking` | Where the bus is right now + route detail |
| Attendance | `/student/attendance` | Teacher-marked class attendance + bus boarding history |
| Exams | `/student/exams` | Upcoming exams |
| Results | `/student/results` | Published exam results and grades |
| History | `/student/history` | Past trips |
| Notifications | `/student/notifications` | Alerts (bus started, approaching, etc.) |
| Profile | `/student/profile` | Account details |

## Example — Track the bus this morning

1. Open **Live tracking**.
2. You'll see:
   - the **bus** moving live on the map,
   - the full **route** drawn as a line with every **stop** marked,
   - **your stop** highlighted so you know exactly where to wait,
   - the **current/next stop** and an **ETA** in the route track panel.
3. When the bus is approaching your stop, you get a notification (if enabled).

> If no trip is running, the page shows the route and stops with an idle status until the driver starts the trip.

## Example — Review attendance

1. Open **Attendance**.
2. The top section is your **class attendance** — marked by teachers in class (present/absent/late) with an
   overall attendance rate. This is your official attendance.
3. The **bus boarding history** below shows pickup/drop-off check-ins recorded on the bus. It is informational
   only and does not count as class attendance.

## Notifications

Typical alerts: `BUS_STARTED`, `BUS_APPROACHING` (bus reached your stop's geofence), `STUDENT_PICKED`
(driver checked your child in), `NO_SHOW` (bus passed your stop without your child boarding), `STUDENT_DROPPED`,
`TRIP_COMPLETED`, and general school notices. Mark them read from the Notifications page.

## Tips

- Add the app to your home screen for quick morning checks.
- If tracking is empty, the bus trip may not have started yet, or a student–bus assignment may be missing —
  contact your school admin.
